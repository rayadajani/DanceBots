DANCE BOTS!

Students learned about defining a function, using loops, and variables in order write code that will enable the robot to dance to. The Dance Battle was one of the several phases that the students had to complete throughout the Go Pi Go Robot project. For the Dance Battle phase, teams had to create a sequence of python code that followed each beat of a song. Every team got to choose their own song, they were required to make the robotâ€™s dance last for a minimum of 30 seconds. The first round consisted of the students placing their robots on the dance floor, after their robot finished the dance, the dance was scored out of 10 by the teacher. There was a second round a few weeks after, this was called the advanced dance challenge. This was where students were required to create an additional dance move to their original dance sequence. They were required to use loops and variables to call to certain functions. Then, they were also scored out of 10 for the advanced dance.
